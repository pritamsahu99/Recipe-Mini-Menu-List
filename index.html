    3. All Time Favourite - Gulab Jamun

    To make Gulab Jamun, you need to:

    1. Mix mashed khoya with chenna, soda bicarbonate, maida, elaichi powder and water to make a dough
    2. Make multiple balls from the dough of the jamun size
    3. Mix sugar with equal water to make a sugar syrup and keep aside on low heat
    4. Heat the desi ghee in a pan.
    5. Deep fry the jamun balls in desi ghee on low heat until golden.
